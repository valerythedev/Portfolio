Music, technology, and entrepreneurship are the driving forces behind my life's journey, with a strong background in contemporary music. As an entrepreneur, I thrive on creativity and have brought innovative ideas to life. Having moved to the United States at 18, I learned to become independent and self-sufficient in my pursuit of education and personal growth. My passion for this industry stemmed from technology playing an instrumental role in my learning to navigate this new and complex environment upon first moving to the US. My mission is to help others find their way into the tech industry and make it more accessible. Also, as an instrumentalist, I am constantly seeking new ways to express myself and connect with others through the universal language of music. Through perseverance and a commitment to following my passions, I have found great success and continue to seek out new challenges and opportunities to learn and grow.
